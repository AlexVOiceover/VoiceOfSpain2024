Welcome to Voice of Spain! I'm Javier Fernández Peña, an internationally renowned Spanish voice-over artist with 25 years of experience. Known for voicing Spanish Buzz in "Toy Story 3," I bring passion, professionalism, and authenticity to commercials, narrations, and characters. Let my rich, resonant tones bring your project to life.
Based in London, I have the privilege of working with top-tier post-production studios on projects ranging from international commercials to blockbuster movies. This experience has enabled me to experiment with a vast array of equipment, ultimately crafting a studio perfectly tailored to enhance my voice's unique qualities.
